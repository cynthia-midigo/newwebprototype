Does your team need a Presentation Skills Training?

Poor presentation skills in content, design and delivery mean leaders fail to inspire teams, products don’t sell and startups find difficulty pitching to raise funding. My Growth Communication’s workshops and short courses will leave participants with proven processes to transform their boring corporate presentations into riveting visual stories.

Training formats vary according to your company’s unique requirements:

Luncheon Seminars

Get a crash-course introduction to developing effective presentations and visual literacy skills in just a few hours! Participants get a taste of our full program.

Full-day, Hands-on Workshops

These courses teach from the ground-up the principles and methodology we use to persuasive presentations from scratch for top executives, My Growth Communication way.

Customized Master Classes

We create tailored presentation skills course curriculums for your organization so they learn the skills that matter most in the quickest way possible.

Learn from expert presentation trainers that walk the talk

Never in a My Growth Communication training will you find an instructor that only teaches theories. Our trainers are experienced industry practitioners that live and breathe the topic on a daily basis ensuring your teams get only the most relevant education.

Unique Frameworks

All course learners receive proven templates and worksheets including our ‘5P* methodology’ for developing persuasive presentations

Hands-On Exercises

Learners get to apply techniques they learn immediately at work to internalise the lessons garnered during our presentation skills courses.

Real-World Case Studies

Our instructors practice methods they teach everyday in their line of work. Learners will get professional feedback on progress and how to improve.
